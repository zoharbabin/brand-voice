# Brand Guidelines

## Persona

Acme connects business data to the decisions that matter. We write for technical teams that can't afford mistakes — no hype, no shortcuts, just reliable information that scales.

Write like you're explaining something to a sharp colleague. Direct, honest, clear. Second person ("you"). Skeptical customers deserve skeptical writing.

## Tone & Voice

- Direct, honest, clear
- Person: second
- Voice: active
- Sentences: max 25 words
- Contractions: yes
- Exclamation marks: no

## Vocabulary

**Always use:** Acme, Acme Platform, APIs, connect, route, ingest, sync

**Avoid:** leverage, utilize, synergy, seamless, innovative, cutting-edge, revolutionary, best-in-class

**Forbidden:** simply, easy, just, please don't hesitate

## On-Brand Examples

> Connect your data in minutes — Acme handles the routing, you handle the insights.

> When something breaks, you'll know before your customers do.

> Teams using Acme catch pipeline issues 48 hours earlier. We can show you how.

## Off-Brand Examples

> Leverage our cutting-edge platform to seamlessly integrate your revolutionary workflows.

> Acme makes it easy. Simply utilize our innovative APIs.

## Formatting Rules

- Heading style: sentence case
- Oxford comma: yes
- Lists: bullets for unordered, numbers for steps
- Bold: reserve for key terms and callouts
- Readability target: 8th grade

## Quick Reference

1. Active voice — "Acme secures your data," not "your data is secured."
2. No forbidden terms: simply, easy, just, please don't hesitate.
3. Sentences under 25 words.
4. No hype: avoid leverage, utilize, seamless, cutting-edge.
5. Second person. Address your reader as "you."
